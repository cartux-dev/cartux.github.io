Our Story

CARTUX began as a small tea kiosk in Kakanad, Ernakulam, with a vision to serve premium quality tea to busy professionals and locals alike. Our passion for tea and commitment to quality has made us a favorite spot for tea lovers.

We're proud of our Kerala roots and incorporate local flavors and traditions into our offerings. As we grow, we remain committed to providing the best tea experience at affordable prices.

Our future plans include expanding into e-commerce to bring our premium tea products to customers across India.
